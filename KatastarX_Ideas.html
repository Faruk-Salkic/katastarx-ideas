01
Helping Buyers
Features that make buying and renting better, faster and safer.
12 ideas
Verified Badge
MVP

Njuškalo is full of fake listings and everyone knows it. If someone sees a KatastarX Verified badge they immediately trust the listing more. We manually check the title deed against the OIB and put the badge on. Not complicated but the effect is huge.

Note: Verified listings could require a paid monthly subscription to maintain badge status.
Buyers Trust
Aktivna Plomba Warning Tag
MVP

Buyers are constantly scared about hidden legal disputes and nobody explains anything. We let the seller tick a checkbox when listing and show a visible warning tag on the property. Total transparency. People will trust us for this.

Note: Depends on how far we get with e-Gradjani / e-Vizitori integration. Even as a manual checkbox it adds real value.
Buyers Croatian specific Trust
Price Drop Alert
MVP

People check their phones immediately when they get this notification. You saved a property two months ago and suddenly you get a message that the price dropped 15.000 EUR. You open the app right away. One of the strongest features to bring people back without spending anything on marketing.

Note: Need to check if our current alerts and favorites already handle this or if it needs to be built separately.
Buyers Retention
Polygon Map Search
Phase 2

On Njuškalo you draw a circle on the map, that is basically it. We let people draw any shape they want. If you only want to live between two specific streets in Zagreb you can do that. Sounds small but people who know exactly where they want to live go crazy for this.

Note: Might be expensive to implement. Worth researching the cost before committing.
Buyers Search
Smart Exclude Filters
MVP

Right now on Croatian portals you can filter what you want but you cannot filter what you do not want. Let people say no basement apartments, no first floor, no properties with active legal issues. One extra filter option but it saves serious buyers a lot of time.

Buyers Search
Price History Timeline
Phase 2

Show when the listing first appeared, what the price was, and every drop since then. Agents in Croatia hide this to protect sellers but buyers want it badly. We show it. Buyers trust us. Agents might be annoyed but their clients still find us because buyers go where the data is.

Note: Two-edged sword. Good for buyers, might slow some agent partnerships early on. Worth a team discussion.
Buyers Transparency
Currency Toggle
MVP

One button to switch between EUR, GBP, CHF, USD with live rates. Foreign buyers do not want to open a calculator every time they see a price. The ones buying on the coast often have Swiss or German bank accounts. This removes friction immediately.

Buyers International
Collaborative Wishlist
Phase 2

Couples buy apartments together. Let them invite each other to a shared saved list where both can rate and comment on properties. The side effect is that the second person now also creates a KatastarX account. Basically free user acquisition built into the product.

Buyers Growth Acquisition
Foreign Buyer Guide Page
MVP

One content page explaining OIB, reciprocity laws, what a Javni Biljeznik is, typical taxes. Foreigners are completely lost with Croatian bureaucracy and nobody is helping them. We make this page and every foreign buyer searching how to buy property in Croatia finds us first on Google.

Buyers SEO International
Tourist ROI Calculator
Phase 2

Most coastal apartments are bought as Airbnb rentals, everyone knows this. We put a simple calculator on each listing. Enter purchase price, see estimated seasonal rental income. People will spend 30 minutes playing with this. Perfect for diaspora and foreign investors who do not know the Croatian market.

Note: Similar tools exist on California platforms. Could roll out when coastal listings are solid.
Buyers Investors Engagement
KatastarX Estimate (Price Tool)
Phase 3

Like Zillow's Zestimate. Show an estimated market value for every property based on comparable listings nearby. Homeowners will check this constantly even when they are not selling. Turns us from a listings portal into something people visit every month just to see what their apartment is worth.

Note: Reference zillow.com/zestimate — worth studying how they present it. Needs solid data first.
Buyers Retention Data
Document Template Library
MVP

A few downloadable Word and PDF templates for standard Croatian real estate. Pre-contract for reservation deposit (Kapara), checklist for Notary appointment. Static files, zero maintenance. People will bookmark the site just for these.

02
Helping Sellers
Features that help private sellers and agencies list better and understand performance.
6 ideas
Seller Weekly Stats Email
MVP

A basic automated email every Monday. Your listing got 47 views this week and 3 people saved it. That is it. Sellers feel the platform is working for them. And if numbers are low they will buy a Boost to improve visibility. Costs nothing to build, helps sell the subscription.

Sellers Retention Revenue
Listing Quality Score
MVP

Give every listing a score out of 100. More photos, add floor plan, fill in all details, score goes up. Low score listings look worse next to high score ones so sellers fix them on their own. The whole platform ends up looking better than any competitor, basically for free.

Note: Could also add a filter for buyers to only show listings above a certain score threshold.
Sellers Platform Quality
Developer Project Pages
MVP

Dedicated premium pages for new construction projects. Floor plans, availability table, construction timeline, pre-sale interest form. Developers need early reservations to get bank financing and they will pay well for a page that generates those leads. One of our highest revenue plays. Mirza also mentioned this before.

Sellers Growth Revenue
Construction Progress Tracker
Phase 2

For people who bought off-plan. They log in and see Foundation done, walls Q3, keys expected Q1 2027. Developer updates it themselves. It keeps pre-sale buyers coming back to the platform every month which is exactly what we want.

Sellers Buyers Retention
Agent Profile Pages
Phase 2

Each agent gets their own SEO-optimized page with active listings, sold history, response time and client reviews. We can sell paid badges like Top Responder, Verified Pro, 10+ years experience. Sellers searching for an agent on Google find them and land on KatastarX, which means more traffic for us too.

Note: Once an agent has a strong profile page on KatastarX, it often ranks better than their own website. That creates lock-in over time.
Sellers Growth SEO
Instagram Ready Export for Agents
Phase 2

One click and the agent gets a nice square image of the listing with key stats and our logo, ready to post on Instagram or Facebook. Agents post it, their followers see KatastarX, we get free brand exposure. We become part of their daily workflow and they have a real reason to stay subscribed.

Boosting KatastarX
Features that grow the platform, drive traffic, increase revenue or build long-term advantage.
8 ideas
EN/DE Language Toggle
MVP

Around 30 percent of coastal buyers are Germans, Austrians, or diaspora from Germany. None of our competitors care about them. Njuškalo translation is a joke. When we start promoting this should be a key message — we are the only Croatian real estate platform that actually works well in English and German.

Growth International SEO
WhatsApp Contact Button
MVP

A WhatsApp link directly on every listing. One tap and the buyer is talking to the agent. Croatians use WhatsApp for everything including business. Agents will love us because they actually get responses instead of waiting for people to fill in contact forms.

Note: Need to check cost of integration, whether it affects platform security and if it needs any special setup.
Growth Conversion
XML/API Bulk Upload for Agencies
Done

Already implemented. Big agencies have hundreds of listings and are not entering them one by one manually. This was a must for onboarding serious agencies from day one.

Growth Agency Onboarding
"14 People Saved This" Counter
MVP

A small social proof counter on hot listings. Completely honest, just pulls from the database. Buyers see this and feel urgency. Agents love it because it makes their listings look desirable. Takes maybe a day to build.

Note: Need to make sure the numbers cannot be gamed or faked.

Phase 2

We already have a mobile app. This is specifically for agents on desktop. They sit at their computer all day. If KatastarX is installed as a desktop app they open it like email every morning. It becomes a habit. No App Store, no separate development, comes from the web build automatically. Works on Chrome and Edge which covers most Croatian agency computers.

MVP

The codebase should be structured so that legal terms, currencies, measurements and language can be swapped per country easily.
